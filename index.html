Welcome to Genesis

Genesis is a free and open-source software for Rocket League agents with AI. It can play against other agents and be used in ranked/online matches without the need for RLBot.org or RocketHost.

Genesis is designed to be easy to use and to provide a good balance between AI and human control. It can be customized to fit the needs of your imagination.
